## My Learning Journey with BrightLight Data Analytics

I enrolled in the BrightLight Data Analytics Programme, a comprehensive 3-month online course designed for beginners with no prior coding experience. The programme was structured to provide both theoretical foundations and practical skills in data analytics, with evening classes that fit perfectly into my schedule as a working professional. 

## Why I Joined
I wanted to strengthen my ability to make data-driven decisions and gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI. BrightLight offered a clear roadmap from fundamentals to advanced visualization, which aligned with my career goals in energy planning and analytics.

## Key Highlights of My Journey
- Module 1: Introduction to Data Analytics
Learned the role of data analysts and how data informs business decisions.
- Module 2 & 3: Data Collection, Storage, and Wrangling
Practiced cleaning and transforming raw data, and mastered SQL queries for data extraction.
- Module 4: Statistical Analysis
Applied descriptive and diagnostic techniques to uncover trends and correlations.
- Module 5 & 6: Visualization
Built charts and dashboards in Excel and Power BI, focusing on clarity and storytelling.
- Module 7: Portfolio Development
Created a professional portfolio showcasing real-world projects, including interactive dashboards.

## Skills I Gained
- Data cleaning and preparation
- SQL for data manipulation
- Data wrangling and exploratory analysis
- Advanced visualization in Excel and Power BI
- Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Ethical thinking and data governance

## Tools I Worked With

## Impact on My Career
This programme gave me the confidence to tackle complex datasets and present actionable insights to stakeholders. It also helped me build a strong foundation for future roles in data analytics, business intelligence, and energy planning.
